PROJECT MAINTENANCE

Baktier Ahmed Rony & Associates, the revenue firm is founded by Baktier Ahmed Rony – TAX, VAT and Customs Practitioner (ITP), National Board of Revenue and also the Member of TAXES Bar. It focuses on building long-term relationships with their clients and helps them to achieve their business goals, by giving excellent service on VAT, Income TAX, and Customs. Baktier Ahmed Rony & Associates also provides accurate and sound advice for any legal challenges. Their Team of seasoned Barristers, Advocates and Solicitors have more than 10 years of diversified and high-in demand practice in the areas corporate and commercial, general litigation, civil and criminal as well as syariah law, VAT, Tax, and custom having served both the institution and individuals, locally and internationally.
